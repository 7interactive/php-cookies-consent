Client side

All process you can make in 4 steps.

Step 1 - link .js and .css

CookiesConsent.js, cookiesConsent.css

<link href="./styles/cookiesConsent.css" type="text/css" rel="stylesheet">

<script src="./js/cookiesConsent.js"></script>

Step 2 - inicialize CookieConsent

<script>
cookiesConsent = new CookiesConsent();
cookiesConsent.init();
cookiesConsent.setDefaultLang('cs')
</script>

Step 3 - upload config.json

Upload your config.json to folder config.

Step 4 - tag your scripts

Set your script to type="text/plain" and set data-cc_category="{{YourCategoryOfScript}}"
